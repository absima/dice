PickOne Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

PickOne is a simple random picker. It does not require an account, and it does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or other personal information.

Information We Collect

PickOne does not collect personal information. The app runs in your browser and performs random choices locally on your device.

Local Inputs

If you add names to the wheel, those names are used only inside the app interface for that session. They are not sent to a server by PickOne.

Audio

PickOne includes optional sound effects. The sound setting only controls playback in the app and is not used to identify you.

Third Parties

PickOne does not include advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or social media trackers.

Children

PickOne is a general utility app. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
