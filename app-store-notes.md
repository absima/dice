## Short Description
PickOne lets you spin a name wheel, roll a dice, or pick a random number from your own range.

## Longer Description Draft
PickOne helps you make quick random choices with three simple tools in one place: a colorful name wheel, a dice roller, and a number shuffler.

Add up to 30 names to the wheel and spin to randomly choose one. Roll a classic dice when you need a quick 1 to 6 result, or enter your own minimum and maximum values to pick a random number from that range.

Use it for classroom picks, games, small decisions, random numbers, and quick fair choices. No account is needed, and the app keeps the experience clean and focused.
